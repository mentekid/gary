# The Forgetful King

## Campaign Overview

**Level range:** 1-5
**Duration:** 10-15 sessions
**Themes:** senility, whimsy, quirky

## Core Premise

As time goes on, even great men become... less great. King Forthun Greatback invites the party to help him recover his scepter: It's important because there's a ceremony at the end of the month for his granddaughter's wedding, and he can't officiate the union without his scepter. Only, where could it be?
