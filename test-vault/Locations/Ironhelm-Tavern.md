## Summary

The Ironhelm Tavern is the most popular adventurer's gathering spot in the capital. Run by [[Thorin-Ironforge]], it serves as a hub for quest-givers, rumor-mongering, and strong dwarven ale.

## Description

The Ironhelm is a sturdy two-story stone building with a thatched roof. A large iron helmet hangs above the door as a sign. Inside, the common room features a massive fireplace, heavy wooden tables, and walls decorated with trophies from adventurers past.

## Atmosphere

- Always busy in the evenings
- Mix of locals and travelers
- Music from a corner bard most nights
- Smell of roasted meat and spilled ale

## Notable Features

- The Notice Board: Where quest postings appear
- The Trophy Wall: Memorabilia from famous adventures
- Private rooms upstairs for rent
- Cellar with the finest dwarven ale

## Regular Patrons

- [[Thorin-Ironforge]]: Owner and barkeep
- Various adventuring parties
- Off-duty city guards
- Merchants and travelers

## Rumors Heard Here

- The king's behavior has been stranger than usual
- Bandits on the mountain pass are getting bolder
- Something magical was stolen from the palace
