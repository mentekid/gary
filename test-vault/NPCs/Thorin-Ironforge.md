## Summary

A grizzled dwarven fighter with a magnificent beard. Thorin runs the Ironhelm Tavern and serves as a quest-giver for adventurers. He's known for his direct manner and unwavering loyalty to his friends.

## Appearance

Thorin stands 4'6" tall with broad shoulders and calloused hands from years of smithing and combat. His iron-gray beard is braided with copper rings, and his eyes are a piercing blue. He typically wears well-worn leather armor and keeps a warhammer within easy reach behind the bar.

## Personality

- Direct and honest, sometimes to a fault
- Fiercely protective of those he calls friend
- Has a soft spot for underdogs
- Enjoys a good ale and better company

## Relationships

- Close friend of [[Grimble-Tinkertop]]
- Respects [[Elara-Moonwhisper]] despite their differences
- Served under King Forthun in his younger days

## Quest Hooks

- Needs help clearing out bandits on the mountain road
- Has information about the king's missing scepter
- Can craft masterwork weapons for the right price
