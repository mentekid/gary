## Summary

An eccentric gnome artificer known for his wild inventions and magical contraptions. Grimble's workshop is a chaotic maze of gears, crystals, and half-finished projects. He's brilliant but scatterbrained.

## Appearance

Grimble is 3'2" with wild white hair that sticks up in all directions, likely from repeated small explosions. He wears a leather apron covered in tool loops and pockets. His goggles have multiple lenses that flip down for detailed work. Soot stains are permanent features on his hands and face.

## Personality

- Enthusiastic about any new problem or puzzle
- Gets distracted easily by shiny ideas
- Generous with friends, forgetful with enemies
- Talks to his inventions like they're people

## Inventions

- "The Whatsamajig" - nobody knows what it does, including Grimble
- Self-propelled cleaning broom (mostly works)
- Goggles of True Seeing (need some debugging)
