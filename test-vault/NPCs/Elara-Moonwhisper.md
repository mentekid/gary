## Summary

An ancient elven wizard who serves as the king's advisor. Elara is wise, patient, and deeply concerned about the king's declining mental state. She seeks adventurers who can discreetly help with the kingdom's problems.

## Appearance

Elara appears ageless, with silver hair that flows past her shoulders and eyes like starlight. She wears flowing robes of deep purple embroidered with arcane symbols. A staff of twisted oak never leaves her side.

## Personality

- Wise and thoughtful, considers all angles
- Patient but can be stern when necessary
- Values knowledge and diplomacy over force
- Protective of the kingdom and its people

## Relationships

- Trusted advisor to King Forthun for over a century
- Maintains correspondence with [[Grimble-Tinkertop]] on magical matters
- Respects [[Thorin-Ironforge]] despite different approaches

## Secrets

- Knows the king's condition is worsening
- Has theories about what really happened to the scepter
- Worries about succession and stability

## Quest Hooks

- Needs discrete investigation into palace affairs
- Seeks rare magical components
- May have information about ancient prophecies
